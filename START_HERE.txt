Q: I don't have embeddings yet. What do I do?
A: Extract them first using CLIP, ResNet, or any vision model.
   See QUICKSTART.md for examples.

Q: Will this work with my specific dataset?
A: Yes! It's designed to work with ANY domain and ANY size.

Q: Do I need pre-computed clusters?
A: No, they'll be computed automatically if not provided.

Q: What if I don't have CLIP installed?
A: The system will work with basic embeddings. CLIP is optional
   but recommended for better results.

Q: How accurate is it?
A: Depends on your embeddings quality. Typical confidence: 0.6-0.9.
   The system assigns ALL images - check confidence scores in output.
